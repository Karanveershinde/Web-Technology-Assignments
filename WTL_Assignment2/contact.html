Frequently Asked Questions

How can I track my application status?

You can track your application status by visiting the respective service portal and entering your application reference number. Most services provide real-time status updates.

What documents are required for most services?

Common documents include Aadhaar card, PAN card, address proof, and identity proof. Specific requirements vary by service. Check the individual service page for detailed requirements.

How long does it take to process applications?

Processing times vary by service type. Digital services are usually processed within 24-48 hours, while document-based services may take 7-30 days depending on verification requirements.

Can I apply for services on behalf of someone else?

Yes, you can apply on behalf of family members with proper authorization documents. Some services may require the applicant to be physically present for verification.

What should I do if I face technical issues?

For technical issues, try clearing your browser cache, using a different browser, or contact our technical support team at 1800-11-1234. Our team is available 24/7 to assist you.
